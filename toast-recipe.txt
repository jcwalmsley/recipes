1. pre-heat oven to 400 deg F
2. apply thin layer of margarine or butter to bread
3. apply sugar or cheese above the topping just added in previous step
4. if using sugar add a light cover of powdered cinnamon 
5. Once oven reaches desired temperature insert baking tray with bread prepared in accordance with the above steps
6. Remove the baking tray when the sugar or cheese has melted for about one and a half to two minutes
7. Turn off oven and let toast cool for four minutes before serving
